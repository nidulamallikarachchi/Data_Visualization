The Correlation Between Tuberculosis Prevalence and Sales of Antibacterial Drugs for Tuberculosis Prevention in OECD Countries


Welcome to Our Data Visualization Website!

Tuberculosis (TB) remains one of the most significant public health challenges worldwide. This website aims to provide an insightful analysis of the relationship between the prevalence of tuberculosis and the sales of antibacterial drugs used for its prevention across OECD countries. Through interactive visualizations, we delve into the trends and patterns that emerge over the years, offering a comprehensive view of this critical issue.

Comparative Sales Analysis of Antibacterial Drugs for Tuberculosis Prevention in OECD Countries (2010-2021)

Radar Chart

In this section, we present a radar chart that compares the sales of antibacterial drugs specifically designed for tuberculosis prevention across various OECD countries from 2010 to 2021. This visualization highlights the differences and similarities in drug sales, providing a clear picture of each country's approach to tuberculosis prevention.

Global Distribution of Tuberculosis Infections Per 100,000 Population (2000-2022)

Choropleth Map

The choropleth map illustrates the global distribution of tuberculosis infections per 100,000 population from 2000 to 2022. By visualizing the data on a world map, we can observe the geographical spread and intensity of TB infections, identifying regions with the highest prevalence and the progress made over the years.

Trends in Sales of Antibacterial Drugs for Tuberculosis Prevention in OECD Countries vs. Global Tuberculosis Infection Rates (2010-2021)

Line Chart

This line chart compares the trends in the sales of antibacterial drugs for tuberculosis prevention in OECD countries with the global tuberculosis infection rates from 2010 to 2021. This analysis helps us understand the relationship between drug sales and infection rates, providing insights into the effectiveness of preventive measures and identifying areas needing further attention.


Questions Answered by our Data Visualizations

What are the trends in sales of antibacterial drugs for TB prevention across different OECD countries?

The radar chart will help users understand how drug sales vary among countries and identify patterns or anomalies in these sales.

How does the prevalence of tuberculosis vary globally, and which regions are most affected?

The choropleth map will provide a visual representation of TB incidence rates worldwide, highlighting areas with higher or lower prevalence.

Is there a correlation between the sales of antibacterial drugs for TB prevention and the prevalence of tuberculosis in OECD countries?

By comparing the radar chart and the choropleth map, users can explore potential correlations between drug sales and TB prevalence.

How have the trends in TB prevalence and antibacterial drug sales changed over time in OECD countries?

The line graph will allow users to examine the temporal trends and see how TB incidence and drug sales have evolved over recent years.

Which OECD countries have the highest and lowest rates of TB prevention efforts as indicated by drug sales, and how does this align with their TB prevalence?

Users can use the radar chart and choropleth map in conjunction to identify countries with significant efforts in TB prevention and assess the effectiveness of these efforts in reducing TB prevalence.
